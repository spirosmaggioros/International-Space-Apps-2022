This website created for educational purposes only , more specifically , about the international space apps hackathon which took place 1-2 october in 2022.Our challenge was to develop an application that uses ISS's amateur radio station datasets to construct and display images of Earth's ionosphere.

How to use the tool :

The files you can upload MUST be in .csv form. After you hit the submit button the algorithm will transform the data that you inserted into an image that you can download or save in your computer.
